Font Style Overview

A font's style significantly impacts the readability and overall look of your text. Different fonts and sizes can convey various emotions and suit different design needs. Below, you'll find a selection of font styles and sizes that you can experiment with to find the perfect fit for your project.

Available Font Styles

  • Arial Sans-serif | Normal weight
  • Georgia Serif | Normal weight
  • Courier New Monospace | Normal weight
  • Times New Roman Serif | Normal weight
  • Verdana Sans-serif | Normal weight
  • Tahoma Sans-serif | Normal weight
  • Impact Sans-serif | Bold weight
  • Trebuchet MS Sans-serif | Normal weight
  • Lucida Console Monospace | Normal weight

Font Sizes

Font sizes can greatly affect the readability and impact of your text. Below are some common font sizes you can use:

  • 12px - Small text
  • 14px - Normal text
  • 16px - Slightly larger text
  • 18px - Heading text
  • 24px - Larger heading text
  • 32px - Display text
  • 48px - Large display text
  • 64px - Extra-large display text
